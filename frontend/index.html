What Tradex does

Every deal starts the same way. Raw trial balances, general ledgers, inconsistent formats, and tight timelines. Tradex standardizes the mechanical work that happens before real judgment begins.

Core capabilities

  • Ingests Trial Balance and General Ledger files
  • Maps and normalizes accounts into a consistent structure
  • Rebuilds a clean Base EBITDA
  • Produces an auditable EBITDA bridge
  • Surfaces potential QoE style adjustments for review
  • Outputs structured, exportable workpapers

Nothing is automated beyond recognition.

Tradex does not replace analysts. It removes unnecessary rework. Human judgment always stays in control.

Outputs are designed to be easy to review, explain, and export into existing workflows.

Why Tradex exists

Most of the early work in financial diligence is repetitive, manual, and rebuilt from scratch on every deal. It is necessary, but it is not where value is created.

Tradex exists to standardize that foundation so teams can spend more time on analysis, discussion, and decision making, not spreadsheet cleanup.

Who Tradex is for

Tradex is built for teams that rebuild EBITDA from raw accounting data.

Teams

  • M&A advisory teams
  • Transaction advisory and financial diligence teams
  • Corporate finance boutiques
  • Investors running hands on diligence

If this is you

If your week one includes mapping accounts, normalizing messy exports, rebuilding an EBITDA base, and preparing buyer ready workpapers, Tradex is designed for your workflow.

How Tradex is used

Tradex is used on live deals. Teams upload financial data, review normalized outputs, apply their own judgment, and export results into their existing processes.

What Tradex is not

  • A marketplace
  • A sourcing platform
  • A replacement for professional judgment
  • A black box decision engine

Tradex is infrastructure. Reliable, neutral, and built to sit quietly under the deal process.

Pricing

Tradex is priced per deal.

This keeps usage aligned with deal economics and avoids long contracts or seat based licensing. Pricing details are shared privately.

Status

Tradex is currently in private use with early transaction teams. We are rolling out carefully to ensure reliability under real deal conditions.
